Installation

To install the extension, click on the icon of your browser below and follow the usual installation step for an extension from the extension page.

Chrome Firefox Vivaldi

Cookie-Editor is available on Google Chrome, Firefox, Firefox for Android and Vivaldi. It will soon be available on Opera.
